About

I am Wendy. I’ve always recognised the joy that being creative brings – it allows me an escape and gives me a lift, a spring in my step. Creating for others allows me to spread that joy and give others a spring in their step.

Starting small and looking to bring joy into the lives of others, my art has been developed using both traditional ink and watercolour as well as using digital drawings onto a surface.

Most of my art is inspired by nature and by the local architecture and older buildings near where I live in Hampshire. I love finding out more about the positive effects of art and nature and look for ways to share this.
